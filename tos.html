Privacy Policy

Scaramouche Bot — Last updated: 2025

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains what data Scaramouche Bot ("the Bot") collects, how it is used, and how it is stored. We take your privacy seriously and collect only what is necessary for the Bot to function.

2. Data We Collect

The Bot collects and stores the following data when you interact with it:

  • Discord User ID — to identify you across conversations
  • Discord Username & Display Name — to address you correctly
  • Message Content — your messages to the Bot, stored as conversation history to enable memory features
  • User Preferences — mode settings such as NSFW mode, romance mode, and proactive messaging toggles
  • Channel & Server IDs — to track which channels are active for proactive messaging

3. How We Use Your Data

Collected data is used exclusively to:

  • Provide conversation memory so the Bot remembers past interactions
  • Store your personal preferences and mode settings
  • Enable proactive messaging features (if opted in)

Your data is never sold, shared with third parties, or used for advertising.

4. Third-Party Services

The Bot uses the following third-party APIs to function. Your messages may be processed by these services:

  • Anthropic (Claude API) — processes your messages to generate responses. See Anthropic's Privacy Policy.
  • Fish Audio — processes text to generate voice responses. See Fish Audio's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Storage

Data is stored in a local SQLite database on the server hosting the Bot. It is not stored in any external database or cloud storage service beyond the hosting platform (Railway).

6. Data Retention

Conversation history is retained indefinitely to enable memory features. You can delete your own data at any time by using the !reset command, which wipes your conversation history from the database.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete your conversation history at any time using !reset
  • Opt out of proactive messaging using !proactive off
  • Opt out of voluntary DMs using !dms off
  • Request complete removal of your data by contacting the Bot owner

8. Children's Privacy

The Bot is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has used the Bot, please contact the Bot owner for data removal.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time. Continued use of the Bot after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

For privacy concerns or data removal requests, contact the Bot owner through the Discord server where the Bot is hosted.
